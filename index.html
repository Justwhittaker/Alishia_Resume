Digital Marketing

Digital marketing is incredibly important these days as the use of the Internet, mobile devices, social media, search engines, and other channels are in high demand. My aim as a digital marketer is to reach consumers easily by being visible to them on their connected devices and to use visually appealing content to gain
more engagement from my audience.

SEM

SEM (Search Engine Marketing) is the process of getting more website traffic by purchasing ads on search engines and optimising views of your business adverts.

SEO

Search engine optimization (SEO) is the process of growing the quality and quantity of website traffic by increasing the visibility of a website or web page to users of a web search engine so that your business listing appears above your competitors for organic results.

Content Creation

Content creation is the process of generating ideas that appeal to your clientele and customers when creating written or visual content around products and services and making that information accessible to your audience as a blog, video, infographic, etc.

Blog

A Blog Provides a Channel of Communication with Customers. This helps your business to build its relationship with itscustomers. Through a business blog, you can present your content in a more casual and personal way, and actually have conversations with your readers in the comments section

Newsletter

A newsletter are tools that can be used to communicate regularly with your subscribers, delivering the information you want in their email boxes and can be used effectively to drive clients to your website to follow specific “call to action” behaviours such as purchasing products.
